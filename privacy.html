Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2024

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how I, Tamsin Taylor (operating as Tamsin Urquhart), collect, use, and protect your personal information as a psychotherapist and data controller.

I am committed to protecting your privacy and complying with data protection laws including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

1. What Information I Collect

Personal Data Collected:

Contact Information:

  • Name, Email address, Phone number (if provided)
  • Information submitted through the contact form

Therapy Records:

  • Clinical notes from therapy sessions
  • Assessment information and relevant history

2. How I Use Your Information

  • Respond to enquiries and conduct therapy sessions
  • Maintain clinical records and process payments
  • Comply with professional and legal obligations

3. Data Retention

Therapy Records: Retained for minimum 7 years after last session (UKCP requirements)

Contact Enquiries: Deleted within 6 months if therapy does not proceed

4. Your Rights Under GDPR

You have the right to:

  1. Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  2. Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  3. Erasure: Request deletion (subject to professional obligations)
  4. Data Portability: Receive data in structured format

5. Contact About Privacy

For questions about this Privacy Policy or to exercise your data rights, contact me via the contact form.

If not satisfied, you may complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO): https://ico.org.uk/
