Social Media & Electronic Information Policy

Last Updated: November 2024

Introduction

This policy outlines my practice regarding social media and electronic information to protect your privacy and our therapeutic relationship.

1. Social Media

I do not accept friend requests from current or former clients on personal social media accounts (Facebook, LinkedIn, etc). This is to protect your confidentiality and maintain professional boundaries.

You are welcome to follow my professional pages, but please be aware that your engagement there may be public.

2. Electronic Communication

Please use email and text messages only for administrative purposes, such as scheduling or changing appointments. These methods are not secure for sharing sensitive personal information.

3. Search Engines

I do not search for clients on Google or social media. My knowledge of you comes from what you choose to share in our sessions.

4. Location Tracking

If you use location-based services on your mobile phone, please be aware that others may surmise that you are a therapy client if you check in at my office location.

5. Questions

If you have any questions about this policy, please feel free to discuss them with me during our sessions.
