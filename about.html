About Tamsin

Based in Wiltshire; Sessions via Zoom

Some moons ago I packed my suitcase and embarked on a journey. I needed solutions and so I went to therapy. Thankfully it was Internal Family Systems therapy. What I discovered was an abundant, lush and peopled world inside of me - a rich tapestry of wisdoms, joys, dreams, fears and pains. In time, I realised that I was the one that I had been searching for and this has made all the difference. Internal Family Systems Therapy continues to colour my life and help me with the challenge of being alive and my training in clinical hypnosis and psychotherapy further supports this love of coming to know our inner worlds and the working of the unconscious life.

My approach is playful, person centred and creative. Whilst I specialise in IFS therapy I may also include other therapeutic approaches. I offer you a relationship which is safe and supportive abiding by the ethics code of the UKCP and shaped by the principles of the IFS model, as outlined below.
