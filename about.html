About me

Some moons ago I packed my suitcase and embarked on a journey. I needed solutions and so I went to therapy. Thankfully it was Internal Family Systems therapy.

What I discovered was an abundant, lush and peopled world inside - a rich tapestry of wisdoms, joys, dreams, fears and pains. In time, I realised that I was the one that I had been searching for and this has made all the difference.

Internal Family Systems Therapy continues to colour my life and help me with the challenge of being alive. My training in clinical hypnosis and psychotherapy further supports this love of coming to know our inner worlds and the workings of our unconscious life.

In my earlier career I was an actress and director (MA.) I bring transferable skills to this work, helping clients connect to parts in a curious, imaginative and courageous way.

I offer you a space that is safe and supportive abiding by the ethical code of the UKCP and shaped by the principles of the IFS model.
