Services

Collaborative, person-centred therapy to support your healing journey

I offer a collaborative approach to therapy, working with you to understand your needs and walk through unknown territories of the heart. Together we gather gifts and release burdens to help with whatever challenge you face. Healing the imagination with the imagination, one step at a time.

My approach is playful, person centred and creative. Whilst I specialise in IFS therapy, I may also include other therapeutic approaches where beneficial. I offer you a relationship which is safe and supportive, abiding by the ethics code of the UKCP and shaped by the principles of the IFS model.

What to Expect in Therapy

IFS therapy is a collaborative process where you remain in the lead of your own healing. My role is to provide a safe, supportive space and guide you as you explore your inner world.

Sessions typically involve:

  • Developing awareness of different parts of yourself
  • Understanding the protective roles these parts play
  • Accessing your core Self - the calm, compassionate leader within
  • Working with parts to release burdens from past experiences
  • Integrating insights into your daily life

The process is gentle and goes at your own pace. You're always in control of what we explore and when.
